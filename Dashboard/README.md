# Regional Sales Performance Analysis & Dashboard – ABC Electronics

## Project Overview
ABC Electronics is a retail company operating across multiple regions and product categories. Management requires a clear, data-driven understanding of sales performance to identify top-performing regions, profitable product categories, and key sales trends.

This project analyzes regional sales data using **Microsoft Excel**, focusing on **data cleaning, exploratory analysis, pivot tables, visualizations, regression analysis, and dashboard creation** to deliver actionable business insights.

---

## Business Problem
ABC Electronics lacks a consolidated view of sales performance across regions and product categories. Without structured analysis and visualization, decision-makers struggle to identify growth opportunities, pricing impacts, and inventory priorities.

---

## Business Objectives
- Identify **top-performing regions and product categories**
- Analyze the **impact of discounts on sales and profitability**
- Monitor key sales metrics for strategic decision-making
- Provide insights to support **marketing and inventory planning**

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains retail sales transaction data with the following key fields:
- Order ID  
- Order Date  
- Region  
- Product Category  
- Sales Amount  
- Quantity Sold  
- Discount (%)  
- Profit  

---

## Methodology

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Standardized region and product category fields using Excel text functions  
- Ensured consistency and accuracy in transactional data  

---

### 2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis
- Filtered sales data by region, product category, and time period  
- Calculated total sales, average discounts, and profit metrics using Excel formulas  

---

### 3️⃣ Pivot Table Analysis
- Created pivot tables summarizing **sales and profit by region and product category**
- Used slicers for dynamic filtering and analysis  

---

### 4️⃣ Data Visualization
- Bar charts to compare total sales across regions  
- Pie charts to show category-wise sales contribution  
- Stacked bar charts to analyze regional and category distribution  

---

### 5️⃣ Regression Analysis
- Performed regression analysis to evaluate the relationship between **Discount (%) and Sales Amount**
- Used scatter plots to visualize the impact of discounting strategies  

---

### 6️⃣ Dashboard Development
- Built an interactive Excel dashboard displaying:
  - Total Sales  
  - Total Profit  
  - Top-performing Region  
  - Best-selling Product Category  
- Enabled dynamic analysis using slicers and filters  

---

## Key Insights
- Certain regions consistently outperform others in total sales and profitability  
- Higher discounts increase sales volume but can negatively impact profit margins  
- Electronics and Furniture categories contribute significantly to revenue  
- Sales performance varies considerably across regions, highlighting targeted growth opportunities  

---

## Business Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategies to balance sales growth and profitability  
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions and categories  
- Improve inventory planning based on regional demand patterns  
- Monitor key sales KPIs regularly using interactive dashboards
